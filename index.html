Original 3D Scan of Hand

In essence, this 3D scan of a woman's hand was combined with the "Flexy Hand" DIY prosthetic hand architecture to create a highly customized 3D printed prosthetic. For practical purposes a 3D scan of the surviving hand could be mirrored.

Silicone Skin Prosthetic Hand 3D Printed Hard Parts

Finger and thumb segments fit inside the cast silicone rubber skin. You can see the retaining posts across the top of the hand where the edges of the silicone rubber skin end.

Internal Mold
